Warm and filling scrambled eggs

A scrambled egg recipe that gets you up in the morning

Ingredients

  • 3 duck eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Turn pan to medium heat. Add oil and butter
  2. Crack two eggs and egg white of 3rd egg into pan after the oil is sizzling
  3. As soon as the egg white has turned opaque, add the 3rd yolk.
  4. Add salt and pepper, scramble for 20 seconds and take off the heat
