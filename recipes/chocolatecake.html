The GOAT chocolate cake recipe

Chocolate cake that will make all other chocolate cakes pale in comparison

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175Â°C. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until well combined.
  4. Gradually pour in the boiling water while stirring the batter continuously. The batter will be thin, but that's okay.
  5. Divide the batter equally between the prepared cake pans.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool in the pans for about 10 minutes.
  8. Transfer the cakes to a wire rack to cool completely.
  9. Once the cakes have cooled, you can frost them with your favorite chocolate frosting.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
