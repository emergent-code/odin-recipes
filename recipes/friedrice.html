Ultimate fried rice recipe

How to make the objectively best fried rice

Ingredients

  • 1 to 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cups cooked white rice (day old)
  • 1-2 eggs beaten
  • 1/2 cup yellow onion (caramelized)
  • 1/2 cup shredded green cabbage
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of diced, cooked chicken
  • 1/4 cup frozen peas & peas
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce, to taste

Steps

  1. Heat wok over medium high heat, when hot add the onion. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes

  3. Add frozen peas and carrots, and garlic; Cook for another 2 to 3 minutes, until the vegetables are crisp tender.
  4. Remove from the wok and set aside. Add the butter to the wok and let it heat for a couple of seconds.
  5. Add the rice and stir. If it starts sticking add more oil or butter as needed.
  6. When rice looks like it has re-hydrated and is getting soft then start drizzling the soy sauce over the rice to color it.
